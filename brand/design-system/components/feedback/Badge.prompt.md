**Badge** — compact status pill. Use for vacancy state ("Vacature open"), certifications ("SNA-gecertificeerd"), or category labels.

```jsx
<Badge tone="green" solid icon="check">Vacature open</Badge>
<Badge tone="dark" icon="badge-check">SNA-gecertificeerd</Badge>
<Badge tone="red">Productie</Badge>
```

Tones: `red | grey | green | amber | blue | dark`. `solid` fills with the tone colour; default is a soft tint. Icons are Lucide names.
